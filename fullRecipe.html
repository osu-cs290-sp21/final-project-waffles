Chocolate chip cookies

Granny Smith

2 dozen

1 hour 30 minutes

Nothing better than homebaked chocolate chip cookies, made with only the best of ingredients!

  • 1c flour
  • 1/4c sugar
  • 1/2c light brown sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2tsp baking powder
  • 1tsp vanilla extract
  • 1c chocolate chips
  • 1/2c milk
  • 3 eggs
  • butter
  1. Cream together butter, sugar, and brown sugar
  2. Add eggs, milk, and vanilla; combine
  3. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt into a medium mixing bowl
  4. Add dry mix to the butter and sugar, stir still combined.
  5. Add the wet mix to the dry and stir until the dough sticks together in a single mass.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips
  7. Chill dough in the fridge for 30 minutes before scooping onto a baking sheet
  8. Bake at 350 degrees for 12-15 minutes

For an easy sweet treat later, dough can be pre-scooped and frozen for up to 1 month, defrost on the fridge for a day or adding 5 minutes to the baking time.
